# Clinical Trial Figures

Use this reference for clinical trial, cohort, and epidemiology figure work.

## Required Clinical Details

- population and arms
- denominators for each group
- endpoint definition
- time horizon
- effect estimate type
- confidence interval or uncertainty method
- censoring status for survival data

## Figure Types

- CONSORT diagram: enrollment, allocation, follow-up, analysis, exclusions.
- Kaplan-Meier: risk table, censoring marks, time axis, group labels.
- Forest plot: effect estimate, CI, line of no effect, subgroup denominators.
- Adverse events: event category, grade/severity, denominator, absolute counts or rates.
- Timeline/swimmer: patient or cohort trajectories, milestones, censoring or status.
- Diagnostic performance: sensitivity, specificity, ROC, PR, calibration, thresholds.

## Red Flags

- subgroup effects without interaction or prespecified analysis status
- adverse-event rates without denominators
- survival curves without censoring or risk table when available
- relative risk shown without absolute risk context
- endpoint or follow-up window omitted
